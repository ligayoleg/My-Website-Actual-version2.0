About Me

I am a Web Developer/ Programmer from Brooklyn. I am currently a full-time student at New York City College of Technology, majoring in Emerging Media Technologies with focus on Computation and Software Development.
I have always been a visual person. I like movies, cartoons, and virtual media. I wanted to be an animator in the past because it would allow me to create something that people would see, but my focus shifted to web development when I learned that it is possible to create something practical.
My biggest accomplishment was in 2016. I decided to become a civilian after 4 years of active duty in US Navy. A lot of people consider an accomplishment to enlist in the first place, but I think that leaving very structured environment, where the goal is clear, is way scarier than to enlist in the first place.
I am looking for an experience, be it a part-time position, internship, or a project. Being a visual, creative, disciplined and starving for knowledge person, I consider to be a useful member in any team.
